## Why Monsoon Season Hits Your Pipes Hard

Every year between June and September, Arizona homeowners face a unique challenge. The monsoon storms bring intense rain that can overwhelm drainage systems and expose weaknesses in your plumbing.

Here are three things you should check before the first big storm rolls in.

## 1. Clear Your Floor Drains

Dust and debris accumulate in floor drains during the dry months. A clogged drain during a monsoon downpour means water backs up into your garage or laundry room.

## 2. Inspect Your Sewer Cleanout

Locate your sewer cleanout cap outside your home. Make sure it is accessible and not buried under landscaping. During heavy rain, municipal sewer systems can back up, and you need quick access to this valve.

## 3. Check Your Water Heater

Monsoon humidity accelerates corrosion on water heater tanks and connections. Look for rust spots, drips, or green oxidation on copper fittings.
